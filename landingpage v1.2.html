Features

What JSAgriSmart helps you do

Turn noisy market price information into data you can trust. JSAgriSmart collects, cleans and visualizes crop prices in one place, then tailors the experience to each role in the value chain.

Multi-role login

One app that adapts to each stakeholder.

  • Separate, role-based accounts for farmers, managers and retailers.
  • Customized dashboards and permissions for each role.
  • Shared view of key price indicators across the whole chain.

Regional market distribution

See how prices vary across cities and quickly find better deals.

  • Visual comparison of crop prices by region and city.
  • Identify low, medium and high price areas at a glance.
  • Connect sellers and buyers more efficiently along the value chain.
Forecast

Smart price forecasting

Predict upcoming price movements with data-driven models.

  • Forecasts based on historical prices from trusted sources.
  • Scenario views to understand "what if demand shifts".
  • Supports planning for contracts, logistics and inventory.
